First-time setup

1. Go to github.com/settings/tokens?type=beta and create a fine-grained Personal Access Token. Give it permission to Actions: write on this repo only. Set an expiration that works for you (a year is fine).

2. Copy the token (starts with github_pat_ or ghp_) and paste it above. It's stored only in your browser, never sent anywhere except GitHub.

3. Bookmark this page. From now on, just open it and click the big button.

The site is also rebuilt automatically every 4 hours, so this is just for when you want it sooner.
